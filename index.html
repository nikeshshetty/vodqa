About

vodQA, Value Oriented Discussion about Quality Analysis, is a forum that sports the motto - "come, learn something new". So far, we have successfully hosted more than 20+ editions of vodQA across all the ThoughtWorks India offices. The forum offers a unique platform for the software testing industry to strengthen the QA community through knowledge sharing.
